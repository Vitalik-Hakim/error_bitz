## wpf image clip with rounded corners

### Solution

```csharp
<Border CornerRadius="8,0,8,0">
    <Border.Background>
        <ImageBrush Stretch="Fill" ImageSource="ImageSource"/>
    </Border.Background>
</Border>
```
