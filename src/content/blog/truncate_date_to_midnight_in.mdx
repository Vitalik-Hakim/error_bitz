## truncate date to midnight in pandas column

### Solution

```python
df['START_DATETIME'] = pd.to_datetime(df['START_DATETIME']).dt.normalize()
# -or-
df['START_DATETIME'] = pd.to_datetime(df['START_DATETIME']).dt.floor('D')
```
