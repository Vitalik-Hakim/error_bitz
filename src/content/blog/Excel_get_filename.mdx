## Excel get filename

### Solution

```vb
' Excel: cell entry (not VBA but for cells)
=LEFT(RIGHT(CELL("filename");LEN(CELL("filename"))-SEARCH("["; CELL("filename"))); SEARCH("."; RIGHT(CELL("filename");LEN(CELL("filename"))-SEARCH("["; CELL("filename"))))-1)
```
