## how to play ringtone in android

### Solution

```kotlin
val ringtoneUri = RingtoneManager.getDefaultUri(RingtoneManager.TYPE_RINGTONE)
val ringtoneSound: Ringtone = RingtoneManager.getRingtone(requireContext(), ringtoneUri)
ringtoneSound.play()
```
