## vowels in sql

### Solution

```sql
SELECT DISTINCT CITY FROM STATION WHERE SUBSTR(CITY,1,1) IN ('A','E','I','O','U') AND SUBSTR(CITY,-1,1) in ('A','E','I','O','U');
```
